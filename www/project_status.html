Recent Activities

Ben Collins-Sussman is organizing the resolution of various issues related to directory versioning (the so-called "Greg Hudson" issues, because Greg raised them in an email some time ago). Ben is also patiently dealing with lots of little issues: configuration stuff, list watching, tests, various cleanups, etc.

Joe Orton is making properties-over-DAV work; seems to be done, or almost done, now.

Karl Fogel and Mike Pilato finished implementing filesystem deltification, and are now writing various paranoid tests to make sure it never regresses. After that, they will move, with Ben and anyone else who cares to join, on to the rest of the stuff in alpha-checklist.txt.
