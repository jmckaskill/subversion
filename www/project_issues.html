Project: Subversion.

The Subversion community welcomes requests for enhancements and reports of bugs. However, in an attempt to curb the number of issues filed which are

  • duplicates of existing issues,
  • invalid for some reason (e.g. user error), or
  • related not to Subversion, but perhaps to one of the libraries or utilities that Subversion depends upon,
we ask that before filing a new issue in the Issue Tracker, you first send an e-mail to the development list (dev@subversion.tigris.org) fully describing the enhancement or bug that brought you here today. That will give the community a chance to ask questions of you, so that we can fully understand your concern. If your concern is not addressed with a relative immediacy, you will like be asked to return here and file a real issue in the Issue Tracker.

Make sure that your e-mail (and subsequently filed issue) describe your bug or enhancement fully. Provide details about the versions of the relevant software (Subversion, Apache, etc.) that you are using, about your operating system, and any other details that might seem pertinent to the issue. If you can provide a shell script to consistently reproduce a problem, that can be incredibly help to those evaluating and/or working on your issue.
